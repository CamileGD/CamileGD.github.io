Introduction to me

My name is Camile Cazot, I'm a 25 years old game developer, the things I enjoy the most about videogame production is creating interesting mechanics and characters.

Even tho I do have studies in Game Development I am mostly self-taught, I learned to code, draw and design by myself with the sole porpuse of creating the best games that I can create, after all I believe that every game designer should work on games by himself, to learn other areas and understand how hard are things to be better at designing.

A little list of some of my favourite things to know me better; favourite videogame is Enter the Gungeon, my favourite tabletop game is Condottiere, movie is Requiem for a Dream and book is The Count of Monte Cristo.

Professional Work

In Ironhide Game Studio I have worked on 3 different projects.

Kingdom Rush Vengeance - Primal Ravage

Due to some circumstances I was placed as the Lead and sole Designer for an expansion of Kingdom Rush Vengeance 6 months after joining Ironhide.

In the 3 months that we worked on the expansion I was in charge of everything needed for the game from a design perspective; its concept, the story, hero, tower, enemies, stages, texts, mechanics, audios. They were some really hard and intense months but very valuables at the same time, I learned a lot on the project and I am really proud of the work I acomplished on it.

Junkworld

Junkworld is an old project of Ironhide and the one I'm currently working on.

Different to the KR4 expansion, JW is a an advanced project, so there was a lot of balance and re-balance of things already created to do on top of everything else, and I'm learning a lot from it.

Indie Work

Lights Out

Lights out was a game created as a 4-month final project as part of a course during my studies in Video Game Development. We were a team composed of three: an artist, a programmer and me.

My role in this project was doing the game design, part of the art, the technical art aspect and the majority of the programming process. The most interesting aspect of this project was the challenge of making a 2D pixel game in a 3D environment with the goal of having dynamic lights.

Avril Labanshee

Avril Labanshee was created in the frame of "2021 Global Game Jam".

I worked as a designer and programmer, also helped with the game's art a little bit.

Infinity Unlocked

Infinity Unlocked was created in the frame of "2021 Game Jam+" and won its Uruguayan regional.

I worked as the lead game designer, programmer and technical artist.

Souls Tree

Soul's tree is a passion project of our team, this is the project we are currently working on, an autochess roguelike, in which i'm working as it Game Designer and Technical artist.
