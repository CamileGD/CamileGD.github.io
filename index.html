Introduction to me

Hello! My name is Camile Cazot, I'm 26 years old. I love creating videogames, and above all, the thing I love the most is creating interesting mechanics that interlink with each other to form beautiful systems.

I do have studies in Game Development, but I am mostly self-taught, learned to code, draw and design by myself with the sole purpose of creating the best games that I can create. Because I love games.

And here is a little list of some of my favorite things to know me better; my favorite videogames are Enter the Gungeon and Gris, my favorite tabletop game is Condottiere, the movies are Requiem for a Dream and The prestige, lastly the book is The Count of Monte Cristo.

Professional Work

At Ironhide Game Studio I have worked on 5 different projects.

Kingdom Rush Vengeance - Primal Ravage

Due to circumstances, I was placed as the Designer for an expansion of Kingdom Rush Vengeance 6 months after joining Ironhide.

In the 3 months that we worked on the expansion, I was in charge of everything in the design department; Its concept, the story, hero, tower, enemies, stages, dialogues and mechanics. They were some really hard and intense months but very valuable at the same time. I learned a lot on the project.

Kingdom Rush Vengeance - Hammerhold

With better experience than the last time, I was placed as the designer for the last DLC of kingdom rush Vengeance.

I worked on mostly the same aspects as last time but with the experience gained from Primal Ravage and the one I gained after working on Junkworld under my belt.

Junkworld

Junkworld is an old project of Ironhide.

Different to the KR4 expansions, JW was an advanced project, so there was a lot of balance and re-balance of things already created to do on top of everything else. We recently finished it with an expansion.

Undisclosed

I have worked on 2 undisclosed projects, one in my entry year, and one now.

In the first one, I was learning the ropes of the job and what was expected of me, learning how things were done here, doing some level design, character design and balance.

In the second one, I'm working on giving life to an old Ironhide project as its Lead designer.

Indie Work

Souls Tree

Souls tree is a passion project of our team, this is the project we are currently working on, an autochess roguelike, in which I'm working as the Game Designer and Technical artist.

Infinity Unlocked

Infinity Unlocked was created in the frame of "2021 Game Jam+" it's a platform puzzle game with time travel mechanics.

I worked as the lead game designer, programmer and technical artist.

Lights Out

Lights out was a game created as a 4-month final project as part of a course during my studies in Video Game Development. We were a team composed of three: an artist, a programmer and me.

My role in this project was doing the game design, part of the art, the technical art aspect and the majority of the programming process. The most interesting aspect of this project was the challenge of making a 2D pixel game in a 3D environment with the goal of having dynamic lights.
