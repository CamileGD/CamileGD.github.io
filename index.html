Introduction to me

My name is Camile Cazot, I'm a passionate indie game developer, but more importantly, I'm passionate about videogame art in general, not only the graphic aspect of it but also the videogame as an art form itself.

Since finishing my 2 years of study in A+ I struggled a lot to see what I wanted to do, I love game design, and as I said earlier, I love the art in videogames, but what is the art in videogames? Is it the sprites? Is it the code? Is it the music? The possibility of choice? The narration? All of those things and a lot more could be depending of who answer the question, in my case I found beauty in a smooth movement, interesting visuals and a good combination of both, after realizing that I understood what I wanted to be, I want to be a Game designer, but I also want to be a Technical Artist, so I'm going to be both, and this portfolio are the things I'm doing to reach that end goal.

Work

Lights Out

Lights out was a game created as a 4 months final project in the videogame course I did in A+, it was a work of 3 people, one artist, one programmer and me.

In this game I took care of the art design, the level design, the technical art aspect and the majority of the code, the most interesting aspect of this game was how to make a 2D pixel game in a 3D environment with dynamic lights.

Domingos Kombat

This game was a commission done as a side project with the same team that made Lights Out.

My work on this project consisted in mainly the programming of the combat and players, we didn't really have a word in artistic design in this project so we only did the technical part.

Avril Labanshee

Avril Labanshee was created in the 2021 Global Game Jam.

My work on this was primarily designer and programmer, but I also give a hand in the art of the game.
