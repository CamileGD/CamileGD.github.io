Introduction to myself

My name is Camile Cazot, I'm a passionate game developer and I really love videogames and more specifically video games as an art form where you can take the players in an experience crafted by a group of people.

After finishing my 2 year Video Game Development studies in A+, I struggled to find what exactly I wanted to do because I enjoy all parts of the game making process. Game design was a given, I love it, and I think I'm good at it, but being an indie you can't "only" be a game designer, so I filled the role my team needed, that was technical artist. At first I did it because my team needed it, but now I'm in the path of learning how to be a good Game designer/Tech artist because I enjoy both of them very much.

Since I started working in Ironhide Game Studio I have worked on 3 different projects.

Professional Work

Kingdom Rush Vengeance - Primal Ravage

Due to some circumstances I was placed as the Lead and sole Designer for an expansion of Kingdom Rush Vengeance 6 month after joining Ironhide.

In the 3 months that we worked on the expansion I worked on everything needed for the game from a design perspective; the concept, new story, new hero and tower, new enemies, new stages, texts, audio and new mechanics. They were a really hard and intense months but very valuables at the same time, I learned a lot on the project and I am really proud of my work on it.

Junkworld

Junkworld is an old project of Ironhide and the project I'm currently working on.

Different to the expansion JW is a full game, so I have a lot of balance work to do on top of everything else and I'm learning a lot from it.

Indie Work

Lights Out

Lights out was a game created as a 4-month final project as part of a course during my studies in Video Game Development. We were a team composed of three: an artist, a programmer and me.

What was my role in this project? I did part of the art and the level design, technical art aspect and the majority of the programming process. The most interesting aspect of this project was the challenge of making a 2D pixel game in a 3D environment with dynamic lights.

Avril Labanshee

Avril Labanshee was created in the frame of "2021 Global Game Jam".

I worked as a designer and programmer, also helped with the game's art a little bit.

Infinity Unlocked

Infinity Unlocked was created in the frame of "2021 Game Jam+" and won its Uruguayan regional.

I worked as the lead game designer, programmer and technical artist.

Souls Tree

Soul's tree is a passion project of our team, this is the project we are currently working on, an autochess roguelike, in which i'm working as it Game Designer and Technical artist.
